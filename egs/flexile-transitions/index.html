flexile.js transitions

Use the arrows below or the left and right arrows on your keyboard to navigate.

The default transition mode is "vanish". With vanish, transitions occur instantly.

To change the transition mode, simply use the drop-down menu above and then move forwards or backwards through the slides.

There are (currently) 40 transition modes to choose from.

The "fade" mode works by making the top slide completely transparent.

All the other modes move slides across the screen or shrink them.

Some modes (like "spin-1080") should probably be used sparingly.
